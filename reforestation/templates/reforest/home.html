Re-leaf with accountability for sustainability

Join us in being accountable for every tree being planted in our quest to combat climate change. Tree planting process is the easiest step in this journey of reforestation but maintainance to see those trees through to maturity until we achieve the set forest coverage, is our biggest hurdle. Our focus must shift from " how many trees we plant today " to " how many of those trees survive tomorrow ". See our latest updates on tree planting activities by various groups in Kenya, our approach in bringing businesses and individuals on board through marketing and how we connect farmers with institutions, companies and organizations that need tree seedlings and maintainance services.

Summary Report

GreenLead offers the platform to hold the organizations and institutions accountable in their tree planting initiatives by monitoring their activities and providing regular updates on their tree planting initiative. We also give farmers a platform to interact and contact organizations and institutions that need tree seedlings. The local community may be contracted by the different groups to maintain and protect planted trees hence creating job opportunities & good relations.

We also offer a platform for businesses that sell eco-friendly products and services and a commission is charged for the products that are purchased through GreenLead. The accumulated amount collected is then used to roll out several tree planting projects in schools, communal lands etc. Through this, we ensure that for every product or service purchased, a tree is planted in the process.

Routes to Roots

Partnerships

We seek partnerships with environmental organizations, corporate entities, or government agencies that are interested in supporting reforestation and agro-ecotourism initiatives. Through this, we are able to conduct tree planting initiatives through controlled channels and in an accountable manner. These partnerships we enable us to collaborate on carbon offset programs promote agro-ecotourism packages that include visits to reforestation sites.

Marketing

GreenLead as a marketing platform for products that use trees as raw materials and eco-friendly services, ensures that we are all accountable towards this long-term sustainable goal of restoration. Through our marketing strategy, we create a balance between production and consumption as indicated under SDG 12 which demands more efficient and environmentally friendly management of materials across the lifecycle, through production, consumption, and disposal.

Approach

Accountability

To ensure tree seedlings planted are taken care of in order to have a big percentage of them reach maturity for the main goal of inreasing our forest cover

Mobilisation

To actively involve our organizations, institutions, companies, industries and groups

Business

To connect farmers with different groups and individuals for seedlings and periodic maintainance contracts to ensure we are responsible

Education

To involve learning instituitions in tree planting initiatives as a vital activity in teaching the next generations on the importance of protecting our forests

Restoration

Push for businesses that use trees as raw materials to adopt our ' Routes to Roots ' initiative and indirectly be part of the this long journey towards increasing our green spaces.

Lets conduct accountable Reforestation

It is only if the trees we plant reach maturity, then are we going to achieve our main goal of increasing our forest coverage. More often than not, out of all those trees planted only less than half manage to survive and this is ends up becoming a huge setback towards our goal. This happens due to our negligence after tree planting activity and the planted trees are left to the mercies and grace of nature for survival. We give this platform to the various groups in tree planting to register and record on their activities by visualizing the progress they make.

Why register with us

GreenLead also offers the groups an opportunity to participate in the carbon market by selling carbon credits they have generated from the carbon sequestered by the trees they have planted. This provides financial incentives for their reforestation activities. by selling carbon credits, project developers can generate revenue that can be reinvested in sustaining and expanding their reforestation efforts. Learn more about Verified Carbon Standards,carbon sequestration, carbon footprint asssesment and carbon market participation by joining us.
