Accountability Score

At GreenLead, our primary objective is to achieve sustainability through accountability. That's why we have developed a unique accountability scoring system for tree-planting activities. This scoring system measures the difference between a group's initial entry record and their latest update record for the same activity, calculating a percentage that reflects their level of accountability. The higher the percentage, the higher the score, indicating areas such as responsibility, credibility, integrity, and improvement opportunities for the group.
